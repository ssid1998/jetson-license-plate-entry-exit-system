\chapter{Introduction}

Welcome to the Vehicle License Plate Detection System Manual. This manual provides a comprehensive guide for setting up, configuring, and operating a real-time vehicle license plate identification system, designed specifically for use with the NVIDIA Jetson Nano J1010. Built on the powerful Jetson Nano platform, this system utilizes advanced AI capabilities to capture, detect, and display license plate information with impressive accuracy and speed.

This manual will walk you through every step, from installation and configuration to effective use and troubleshooting, helping you achieve optimal performance with your Jetson Nano-based plate detection system. Whether you're aiming for integration with larger projects or simply seeking to experiment with computer vision on embedded platforms, this guide will serve as your go-to resource.
