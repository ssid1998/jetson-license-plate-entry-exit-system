NVIDIA {\bfseries{\href{https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded/jetpack}{\texttt{Jet\+Pack}}}} is a comprehensive SDK for Jetson for both developing and deploying AI and computer vision applications. Jet\+Pack simplifies installation of the OS and drivers and includes the L4T Linux kernel, CUDA Toolkit, cu\+DNN, Tensor\+RT, and more.
