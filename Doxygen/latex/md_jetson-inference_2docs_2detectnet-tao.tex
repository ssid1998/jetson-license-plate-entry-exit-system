 \textsuperscript{Object Detection}\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md212}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Using TAO Detection Models}{Using TAO Detection Models}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md212}
NVIDIA\textquotesingle{}s \href{https://developer.nvidia.com/tao-toolkit}{\texttt{TAO Toolkit}} includes highly-\/accurate high-\/resolution object detection models, optimized/\+pruned and quantized for INT8 precision. jetson-\/inference supports for TAO models that are based on the \href{https://docs.nvidia.com/tao/tao-toolkit/text/object_detection/detectnet_v2.html}{\texttt{Detect\+Net\+\_\+v2}} DNN architecture, including the following pre-\/trained models:\+


\begin{DoxyTable}{3}{}{}{1}
\SetCell{c,bg=\tableheadbgcolor,font=\bfseries}Model  &\SetCell{c,bg=\tableheadbgcolor,font=\bfseries}CLI argument  &\SetCell{c,bg=\tableheadbgcolor,font=\bfseries}Object classes  \\
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/peoplenet}{\texttt{TAO People\+Net}}  &{\ttfamily peoplenet}  &person, bag, face  \\
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/peoplenet}{\texttt{TAO People\+Net}} \textsuperscript{(pruned)}  &{\ttfamily peoplenet-\/pruned}  &person, bag, face  \\
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/dashcamnet}{\texttt{TAO Dash\+Cam\+Net}}  &{\ttfamily dashcamnet}  &person, car, bike, sign  \\
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/trafficcamnet}{\texttt{TAO Traffic\+Cam\+Net}}  &{\ttfamily trafficcamnet}  &person, car, bike, sign  \\
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/facenet}{\texttt{TAO Face\+Detect}}  &{\ttfamily facedetect}  &face  \\
\end{DoxyTable}


Although a section below covers how to load your own TAO models, let\textquotesingle{}s take a look at using the pre-\/trained models first.\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md213}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{People\+Net}{People\+Net}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md213}
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/peoplenet}{\texttt{People\+Net}} is a high-\/resolution 960x544 model with up to \texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}90\% accuracy for detecting people, bags, and faces. It\textquotesingle{}s based on Detect\+Net\+\_\+v2 with a Res\+Net-\/34 backbone. Launching detectnet/\+detectnet.\+py with {\ttfamily -\/-\/model=peoplenet} will run the TAO People\+Net model with INT8 precision on platforms that support it (FP16 otherwise). There\textquotesingle{}s also the {\ttfamily peoplenet-\/pruned} model which is faster and slightly less accurate.

You can also adjust the {\ttfamily -\/-\/confidence} and {\ttfamily -\/-\/clustering} thresholds -\/ these TAO models seem not introduce too many false positives with lower thresholds due to their increased accuracy. \doxylink{requirements_The}{The} \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2webrtc-flask}{Flask webapp}{0} is a convenient tool for playing around with these settings interactively.\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md214}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Dash\+Cam\+Net}{Dash\+Cam\+Net}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md214}
Like People\+Net, \href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/dashcamnet}{\texttt{Dash\+Cam\+Net}} is a 960x544 detector based on Detect\+Net\+\_\+v2 and Res\+Net-\/34. It\textquotesingle{}s intended use is for detecting people and vehicles from street-\/level viewpoints and first-\/person perspectives. Traffic\+Cam\+Net is similar, for imagery taken from a higher vantage point.

\href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tsugHIgFrwI}{\texttt{}}

\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md215}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Face\+Detect}{Face\+Detect}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md215}
\href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/models/facenet}{\texttt{Face\+Detect}} is a TAO model for just detecting faces. It was trained with up to \texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}85\% accuracy on a dataset with more than 1.\+8M samples taken from a variety of camera angles. It has a resolution of 736x416 and uses Detect\+Net\+\_\+v2 with a Res\+Net-\/18 backbone.

\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md216}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Importing Your Own TAO Detection Models}{Importing Your Own TAO Detection Models}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-tao_autotoc_md216}
Although jetson-\/inference can automatically download, convert, and load the pre-\/trained TAO detection models above, you may wish to use a different version of those models or your own Detect\+Net\+\_\+v2 model that you trained or fine-\/tuned using TAO. To do that, copy your trained ETLT model to your Jetson, along with the appropriate version of the \href{https://catalog.ngc.nvidia.com/orgs/nvidia/teams/tao/resources/tao-converter}{\texttt{{\ttfamily tao-\/converter}}} tool. Then depending on your model\textquotesingle{}s configuration (the details of which are typically found on the model card), you can run a script like below to generate the Tensor\+RT engine from the ETLT:\+
