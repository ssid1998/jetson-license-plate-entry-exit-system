New to deep neural networks (DNNs) and machine learning? Take this \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2deep-learning}{introductory primer}{0} on training and inference.

\href{https://github.com/dusty-nv/jetson-inference/blob/master/docs/deep-learning.md}{\texttt{}}

Using NVIDIA deep learning tools, it\textquotesingle{}s easy to {\bfseries{\href{https://github.com/NVIDIA/DIGITS/blob/master/docs/GettingStarted.md}{\texttt{Get Started}}}} training DNNs and deploying them into the field with high performance. Discrete GPUs are typically used in a server, PC, or laptop for training with DIGITS, while Jetson and integrated GPU is used in embedded form factors.

\href{https://github.com/dusty-nv/jetson-inference/blob/master/docs/deep-learning.md}{\texttt{}}

NVIDIA \href{https://github.com/NVIDIA/DIGITS}{\texttt{DIGITS}} is used to interactively train network models on annotated datasets in the cloud or PC, while Tensor\+RT and Jetson are used to deploy runtime inference in the field. Tensor\+RT uses graph optimizations and half-\/precision FP16 support to more than double DNN inferencing. Together, DIGITS and Tensor\+RT form an effective workflow for developing and deploying deep neural networks capable of implementing advanced AI and perception.\hypertarget{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2digits-workflow_autotoc_md250}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{autotoc\+\_\+md250}{autotoc\+\_\+md250}}\label{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2digits-workflow_autotoc_md250}
