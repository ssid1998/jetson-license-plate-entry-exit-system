\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Video4\+Linux2 (V4\+L2) camera capture streaming. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
\doxylink{classgst_camera}{gst\+Camera}, which convieniently handles both V4\+L2 and MIPI CSI cameras, is used mostly in lieu of \doxylink{classv4l2_camera}{v4l2\+Camera}. \doxylink{classv4l2_camera}{v4l2\+Camera} is provided in the event that you would rather interface with V4\+L2 directly, rather than go through GStreamer. 
\end{DoxyNote}
