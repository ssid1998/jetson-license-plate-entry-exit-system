\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\hypertarget{dir_5fcaaf5af391369b2d07f8dcd23285dd_autotoc_md467}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Deploying Deep Learning}{Deploying Deep Learning}}\label{dir_5fcaaf5af391369b2d07f8dcd23285dd_autotoc_md467}
Welcome to our instructional guide for inference and realtime vision DNN library for {\bfseries{\href{https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded-computing}{\texttt{NVIDIA Jetson}}}} devices. This project uses {\bfseries{\href{https://developer.nvidia.com/tensorrt}{\texttt{Tensor\+RT}}}} to run optimized networks on GPUs from C++ or Python, and Py\+Torch for training models.

Supported DNN vision primitives include \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2imagenet-console-2}{{\ttfamily image\+Net}}{0} for image classification, \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2detectnet-console-2}{{\ttfamily detect\+Net}}{0} for object detection, \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2segnet-console-2}{{\ttfamily seg\+Net}}{0} for semantic segmentation, \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2posenet}{{\ttfamily pose\+Net}}{0} for pose estimation, and \doxysectlink{md_jetson-inference_2docs_2actionnet}{{\ttfamily action\+Net}}{0} for action recognition. Examples are provided for streaming from live camera feeds, making webapps with Web\+RTC, and support for ROS/\+\+ROS2.



Follow the {\bfseries{Hello AI World}} tutorial for running inference and transfer learning onboard your Jetson, including collecting your own datasets, training your own models with Py\+Torch, and deploying them with Tensor\+RT.\hypertarget{dir_5fcaaf5af391369b2d07f8dcd23285dd_autotoc_md468}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Table of Contents}{Table of Contents}}\label{dir_5fcaaf5af391369b2d07f8dcd23285dd_autotoc_md468}
