Next, download and extract the trained model snapshot to Jetson. From the browser on your Jetson TX1/\+\+TX2, navigate to your DIGITS server and the {\ttfamily Detect\+Net-\/\+COCO-\/\+Dog} model. Under the {\ttfamily Trained Models} section, select the desired snapshot from the drop-\/down (usually the one with the highest epoch) and click the {\ttfamily Download Model} button.



Alternatively, if your Jetson and DIGITS server aren\textquotesingle{}t accessible from the same network, you can use the step above to download the snapshot to an intermediary machine and then use SCP or USB stick to copy it to Jetson. ~\newline
