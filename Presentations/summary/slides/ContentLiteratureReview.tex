\STANDARD{Description of Application I}
{
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Market Basket Analysis  (\cite{majumdar_apriori_2015}) is a technique used by retailers to understand the purchase behavior of customers. At its core, it uses the Apriori Algorithm. 
		\item It helps retailers to identify the relationship between the items that customers buy together.
		\item Retailers can use this information to optimize their store layout, product placement, and marketing strategies.
		\item Boost sales.
		\item Uncover interesting patterns. People who buy diapers are likely to buy beer.	
	\end{itemize}
