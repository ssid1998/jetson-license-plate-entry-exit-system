\chapter{List of Additional Parts}

While the core License Plate Detection System is self-contained within the reComputer chassis, several external peripherals are required for system configuration, monitoring, and data retention. These "Additional Parts" are essential for the operator interface and must meet the specifications listed below.

\section{Component Selection Notes}
\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{SSD Requirement:} Do not use a standard USB flash drive for the database. The high volume of read/write operations requires the durability and speed of an SSD connected to the \textbf{Blue USB 3.0} port.
	\item \textbf{Input Devices:} Simple, wired peripherals are recommended over Bluetooth devices to ensure compatibility during the boot sequence (BIOS/Bootloader level).
\end{itemize}
