\chapter{Compliance and Safety Guidelines}

\section{Compliance with Standards}
Ensure that your installation complies with local safety standards and that the hardware meets the necessary certifications.

\section{Safety Precautions}
\begin{itemize}[label=--,left=1.5em]
	\item Avoid exposing the Jetson Nano J1010 to extreme temperatures or humidity.
	\item Use a surge protector to protect against voltage fluctuations.
\end{itemize}
