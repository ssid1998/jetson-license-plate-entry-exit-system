\section{Hardware Components}

Each part of the system plays a specific role in making automatic plate recognition work smoothly:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Camera:} Captures live video of vehicles entering or exiting the airport gate.
	\item \textbf{Jetson Nano (reComputer J1010):} The main unit that reads the video, detects number plates, and calculates parking time.
	\item \textbf{Display Screen:} Shows live detection results and timing information.
	\item \textbf{Power Adapter:} Supplies stable 5V/4A power through the USB-C connector.
	\item \textbf{Local Database:} Stores all plate numbers with date, time, and charge status.
\end{itemize}
