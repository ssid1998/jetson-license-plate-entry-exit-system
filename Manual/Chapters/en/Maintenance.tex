\chapter{Maintenance}

Regular maintenance helps ensure the Vehicle License Plate Detection System continues to perform effectively. This chapter outlines essential maintenance tasks.

\section{Hardware Maintenance}
\begin{itemize}[label=--, left=1.5em]
	\item \textbf{Inspect Connections:} Periodically check all connections (power, camera, LED, and GPIO) to ensure they are secure and free of dust or debris.
	\item \textbf{Clean Components:} Gently clean the Jetson Nano, camera, and other components with compressed air or a soft cloth to prevent dust buildup, especially around ventilation areas.
	\item \textbf{Check for Wear and Tear:} Regularly examine cables, connectors, and the LED indicator for any signs of wear, replacing items as needed to prevent malfunctions.
\end{itemize}

\section{Software Maintenance}
\begin{itemize}[label=--, left=1.5em]
	\item \textbf{Update Software:} Periodically update the JetPack SDK, Python packages, and dependencies to benefit from security patches and performance improvements.
	\item \textbf{Recalibrate Camera (if applicable):} If detection accuracy declines, recalibrate the camera settings to ensure it maintains proper focus and lighting adjustments.
\end{itemize}

\section{Model Maintenance}
\begin{itemize}[label=--, left=1.5em]
	\item \textbf{Retrain Model (Optional):} As new license plate styles or formats are introduced, consider retraining the model with updated datasets to maintain high accuracy.
	\item \textbf{Clear Log Files:} Regularly clear log files generated by the system to free up storage and avoid slowdowns.
\end{itemize}

Consistent maintenance helps prolong the life of your system and ensures reliable performance for license plate detection.
