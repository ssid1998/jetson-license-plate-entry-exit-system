	\noindent
	\textbf{Dear User,}\\[4pt]
	Welcome to your \textbf{Smart License Plate Detection System} developed at \textbf{Hochschule Emden–Leer}.  
This device recognises and logs vehicle license plates in real time.
	
	\vspace{0.6em}
	\hspace{0.3em}The system starts automatically when powered on — no setup or configuration is required.  
	Within seconds, it begins detecting and displaying vehicles on screen with accurate entry, exit, and duration details.
	
	\vspace{0.6em}
	\faCogs\hspace{0.3em}\textbf{Key Highlights:}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Works entirely offline — no internet required.
		\item Designed for 24/7 airport gate operation.
		\item Automatically handles billing for stays beyond 15 minutes.
		\item Recognises exempt vehicles (police, ambulance, government).
		\item Compact, efficient, and easy to maintain.
	\end{itemize}
