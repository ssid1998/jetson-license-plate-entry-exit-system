\chapter{Introduction}

In today's fast-paced world, ensuring robust security and efficient authentication mechanisms is crucial to fostering a safer environment. One essential aspect of security is the recognition of vehicle license plates for authorization purposes. Manual monitoring of all vehicles is impractical, especially in busy areas. This challenge underscores the importance of automatic license plate recognition systems, which provide a reliable and efficient solution for vehicle authentication without human intervention.\\

This project falls under the domain of "image processing" and leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), a specialized type of Artificial Neural Network. CNNs are particularly adept at handling complex image-based pattern recognition tasks due to their hierarchical structure and ability to extract features effectively. The proposed solution integrates two key phases to achieve vehicle authorization based on license plate recognition. In the first phase, the system undergoes rigorous training and testing on a comprehensive dataset to identify and extract license plate information accurately. This phase is critical for enabling the system to generalize across different plate designs and conditions. Following the training, the system is deployed on the NVIDIA Jetson Nano platform.\\

In the second phase, the Jetson Nano, equipped with an onboard camera, captures real-time images of vehicle license plates. The system processes the captured data to identify the plate's alphanumeric details. If the recognition is successful and the identified plate matches the authorized database, a green indicator is triggered, granting vehicle access. Conversely, if the plate is unrecognized or unauthorized, a red indicator activates, signaling that access is denied. This approach ensures a seamless and automated solution for managing vehicle authorization efficiently.\cite{Sandha:2017}

\section{Challenges}

Vehicle license plate recognition involves overcoming several challenges, including:\cite{Awalgaonkar:2021}

\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Variability in Plate Design:} License plates exhibit significant variations in design, including differences in colors, fonts, and sizes. This diversity complicates the development of a universal recognition system.
	\item \textbf{High-Speed Vehicles:} Capturing clear images of plates from fast-moving vehicles, such as at toll booths or on highways, poses a significant technical challenge.
	\item \textbf{Varying Plate Sizes:} Differences in plate dimensions across regions and countries add complexity to recognition algorithms.
	\item \textbf{International Traffic:} The system must be capable of recognizing a broad range of license plate formats from different countries in areas with international traffic.
	\item \textbf{Real-Time Processing:} Ensuring that recognition algorithms can operate efficiently in real-time scenarios, particularly in high-traffic environments, demands substantial computational power.
\end{itemize}

\section{Proposed Solution}

The proposed solution employs the YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection framework to address the challenges of license plate recognition effectively:\cite{Sung:2020}

\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Variability in Plate Design:} YOLO enables precise localization of license plates within images. Additional processing, such as Optical Character Recognition (OCR), is used to extract alphanumeric details from plates.
	\item \textbf{High-Speed Vehicles:} YOLO's real-time object detection capabilities make it well-suited for capturing plates of moving vehicles, provided a high-quality camera setup is used.
	\item \textbf{Varying Plate Sizes:} YOLO's adaptability allows it to handle objects of varying scales, making it versatile for recognizing plates of different dimensions.
	\item \textbf{International Traffic:} Training YOLO on a diverse dataset ensures compatibility with a wide range of plate designs and formats from various countries.
	\item \textbf{Real-Time Processing:} YOLO's efficient architecture enables real-time detection, with performance dependent on the hardware configuration, such as the use of GPUs like the Jetson Nano.
\end{itemize}
