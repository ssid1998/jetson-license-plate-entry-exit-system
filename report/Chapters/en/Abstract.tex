\chapter{Abstract}

Current techniques for license plate detection and recognition primarily rely on small, often non-global, and non-generic datasets due to the absence of publicly accessible large  and diverse datasets. In this study, we introduce the Car License Plate Detection Dataset (CLPD), a substantial and comprehensive collection of license plate images. Each image is meticulously annotated with bounding box coordinates. To the best of our knowledge, CLPD stands as the most extensive publicly available license plate dataset to date, comprising more than 433 unique car images and the exclusive provision of vertex location annotations.Leveraging CLPD, we present an innovative Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model capable of predicting bounding boxes and simultaneously recognizing associated license plate numbers swiftly and accurately.Through a series of close-range experiments, our model outperforms current object detection and recognition methods in terms of both precision and speed.\\\\
\textbf{Keywords}: License plate detection · Car License Plate Detection Dataset (CLPD) · Bounding box annotation · Vertex location annotations · Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) · Object detection
